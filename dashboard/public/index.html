Info on how to use the dashboard:

- The points transparency is the ratio of the time spent on each beacon (only in Pause mode).

- You have multiple options in the menu:
 - Beacons. Whether or not the beacons are placed on the map.
 - Seats. Whether or not the users seats are placed on the map.
 - Speed. Change the speed when "Play" is pressed.
 - Interval. The interval is how much the day is divided (from every 30sec to a full day).

- After you choose your day and interval length, you can press "Submit Date & Interval".
All the other options are changed whithout the need to submit.

- The smoothest animation is 30sec Interval with a Normal playing speed (30sec every 125ms).

- Keybord Shortcuts. You can press play/pause with the space bar and navigate through the day with the arrow keys

- If you have any questions/suggestions/bugged functions on how the website works, please send me an email!
